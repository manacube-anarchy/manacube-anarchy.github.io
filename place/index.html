Important Information about the /place event

  • You can do /toggle to disable the double click to place block feature, or to turn it back on
  • You can do /store to buy more blocks
  • One (1) Cubit is approximately equal to one (1) American Dollar (~ 0.90 Euros)
  • One block is anywhere from 11 to 17 cents to buy
  • "First to type" and "First to unscramble" challenges pop up around once a minute. Winning will give one block.
  • A Mana Fairy will spawn every fifteen minutes. Finding it and clicking it will give up to 15 blocks. (Note: You have to be one of the first five people to find it)
  • Unless you buy blocks, find the Mana Fairy, win the challenges, or buy a rank, there is a 60 second cooldown between placing blocks.
  • You can buy ranks to decrease the cooldown for placing blocks.
  • You can view a live view of the whole canvas or watch a timelapse (generated hourly) here.
  • You can view last year's (2023) canvas or watch the end timelapse here.
